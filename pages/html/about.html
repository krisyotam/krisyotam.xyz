About Me

Hello! I am Kris Yotam, a classical educator, lifelong learner, and passionate researcher. My work reflects my commitment to the pursuit of knowledge, virtue, and wisdom. I strive to inspire others through education grounded in classical principles and practical wisdom.

My Mission

My mission is to guide others toward the true, the good, and the beautiful. I believe education should shape not only the mind but also the soul, nurturing a deeper understanding of the world and encouraging lives of purpose and integrity.

By integrating classical education principles, I aim to inspire individuals to think critically, act with purpose, and live according to timeless principles.

Experience

  • Classical Educator and Tutor: Educating students in philosophy, literature, logic, and mathematics with a focus on critical thinking and moral character.
  • Researcher: Conducting interdisciplinary research bridging classical studies and modern thought in areas like philosophy, history, and sciences.
  • Programmer: Developing software solutions to enhance practical and educational endeavors.

My Personal Philosophy

I view education as a tool for moral and intellectual transformation. Guided by the Bible as my ultimate authority, I practice asceticism and minimalism to focus on what truly matters: knowledge, virtue, and faith.

My work reflects my belief in aligning education with the divine wisdom inherent in God's design for the world.

Core Values

  • The Laws of God: Living according to divine laws and guiding others toward righteousness.
  • The True, The Good, and The Beautiful: Upholding classical education's ideals in my work and life.
  • Integrity: Building trust through honesty and transparency.
  • Minimalism: Simplifying life to focus on what truly matters.

Future Plans

My goal is to create high-quality, accessible educational content that combines classical methodologies with modern innovations. By curating flexible learning pathways and crafting self-education guides, I aim to empower learners of all ages to pursue knowledge with curiosity and confidence.
