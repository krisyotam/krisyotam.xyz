My Personal Library

This is a list of the books in my personal library. If you have any questions or want me to review any of them, contact me.

This page is part for the public interest, but I also use it to organize and sort my books in my physical library. They are sorted by the Library of Congress Classification. Without a sorting mechanism, finding a book becomes somewhat difficult.
